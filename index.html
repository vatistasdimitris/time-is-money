🗓️ Week Plan Bot 🤖

Welcome to the Week Plan Bot! 🎉
This AI assistant is designed to assist you in managing your weekly schedule efficiently. 📅

How to Use 🚀

  1. Start a Conversation: Initiate a chat with the Week Plan Bot by typing a message or selecting a predefined command. 💬
  2. Send Your Weekly Plan: Share your weekly schedule with the bot. You can input your availability, appointments, tasks, or any other relevant information for the week. 📝
  3. Receive Insights: Once you've shared your weekly plan, the bot will analyze it to identify your open hours and provide you with insightful suggestions or recommendations. 💡
